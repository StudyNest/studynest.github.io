Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how StudyNest uses cookies, Local Storage, and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website.

1. What are cookies and local storage?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer when you visit a website. "Local Storage" is a similar technology that allows a website to store information locally on your computer. We use both to ensure our website functions correctly and to save your preferences.

2. Types of Technologies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies / Storage

These are required to make our website function properly (such as logging in). They cannot be disabled in our systems.

3. How can I control cookies?

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies via your web browser settings. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though some functionality (like logging in) may be restricted.
