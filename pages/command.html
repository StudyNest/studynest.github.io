Command Words

Definitions and requirements for exam success.

AQA Geography (A-Level)
Assess (9 or 20 Marks) Weigh up the importance of different factors or arguments and come to a conclusion about which is the most significant.
Evaluate (20 Marks) Judge the success, validity, or usefulness of a statement, model, or strategy. Does it work in reality?
To what extent (20 Marks) Form a judgement on how true a statement is. You can agree completely, disagree completely, or agree partially.
Analyze (6 Marks) Break down the data or resource to find connections, patterns, and relationships.
